Before You Go Bankrupt

The Trustee is not your advocate. The Trustee is an intermediary between you and your creditors and therefore cannot prefer your interests over those of your creditors.
.
If there is no prior bankruptcy - and no one opposes your disxcharge from bankruptcy, you will be eligible for discharge in 9 mohtns, if no surplus income is owned or 21 months if surplus income is owed,

Surplus income is any income a bankrupt earns above what the government establishes to be the minimum amount of income needed to satisfy your basic needs.

With a few exceptions - you must surrender your property to the Trustee.


Certain debts such as child and spousal support, student loans and certain judgments will continue to exist after bankruptcy.
