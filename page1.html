Before You Go Bankrupt

Bankruptcy is a legal proceeding.
The Trustee acts for the creditor not for you
If no prior bankruptcy, discharge usually in 9 months unless
 opposition
 - discharge is not automatic and may be opposed by the trustee or creditor.
 or
required to make surplus income payments -then 21 months
 - a bankrupt is permitted to earn minimum income and any amounts in excess is surplus income and a portion must be paid to the trustee.
You must surrender your property to the Trustee except for
- necessary clothing to an amount of $5,650
- business tools to a value of $11,300
- farmers assets equlal to $28,300
- motor vehicle to a value of $5,650
- most pension plans and insurance
Certain debts will exist after bankruptcy
- child & spousal support
- certain judgments
- student loans
